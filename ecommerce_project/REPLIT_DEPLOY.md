# E-Commerce Analytics Platform - Ready to Deploy

Your e-commerce analysis platform is ready to share with friends! Follow these steps to deploy on Replit.

## **Quick Deploy to Replit (2 minutes)**

### Step 1: Prepare Your Code
All files are ready. Just make sure you have:
- ✅ `app.py` (main app)
- ✅ `ecommerce_data.csv` (data file)
- ✅ `requirements.txt` (dependencies)
- ✅ `templates/` folder (HTML files)
- ✅ `static/` folder (CSS/JS files)

### Step 2: Upload to Replit
1. Go to https://replit.com
2. Sign up (free account)
3. Click **"Create"** → **"Import from GitHub"** or **"Upload files"**
4. Upload this entire `ecommerce_project` folder

### Step 3: Run It
1. Replit will detect `requirements.txt`
2. Click the **"Run"** button
3. Your app starts automatically
4. You get a public URL (looks like: `https://projectname.username.repl.co`)

### Step 4: Share with Friends
Copy the Replit URL and share with friends - they can access it immediately, no installation needed!
